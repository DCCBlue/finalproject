What is Valorant?

Valorant is a 5v5 tactical shooter game developed by Riot Games. The game was officially released on June 2, 2020. Valorant is a game that requires teamwork, communication, and strategy to win. The game is set in a near-future Earth, where players play as "Agents" with unique abilities. The game is played in rounds, where one team are the "Attackers" and the other team are the "Defenders". The goal of the game is to plant the "Spike" (a bomb) on the "Spike Site" and defend it until it explodes. The game has a variety of weapons, abilities, and maps to choose from. Valorant is a game that requires skill, precision, and quick thinking to outsmart your opponents and win the game.

What makes Valorant a unique tactical shooter is Riot Games' ability to combine the traditional FPS and put a spin on it with unique aspects to the genre. Following a formula from its major competitor "CS:GO", Riot Games' take on tactical shooters cultivated many veteran and new FPS players. The design of the game is built on a solid foundation of FPS fundamentals we love and know, while still including fresh ideas to set itself apart from its competitors. Because of Riot Games' innovative addition to future FPS games and having a notable reputation from their golden children League of Legends and Teamfight Tactics, Valorant has been able to become a leading FPS game since its release in 2020.
